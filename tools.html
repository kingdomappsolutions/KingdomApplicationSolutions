Online Software Tools Designed to Aid Your Efforts for the Kingdom!

Kingdom Application Solutions LLC offers a comprehensive online software tool along with the development of mobile apps, websites, and graphic design. This tool is designed to bring you deeper into a relationship with Jesus Christ and gives you the ministry system tools to lead others deeper as well. We are to grow in Christ-likeness and teach others how to do so as well (2 Timothy 2:2). Online software tools such as growth fundamentals, disciplemaking tools, studies in theology and apologetics, learning about your spiritual gifts, and how to lead and multiply small groups give you the ability to make disciples who make disciples.


This online software tool will be available through a subscription plan (billed annually). The Kingdom Ministry Systems tool is currently in development and will be released at a later time as well as other potential online tools. Listed below are the components that make up the Kingdom Ministry Systems tool and the subscription packages Kingdom Application Solutions LLC offers to which you can subscribe.


Tool in Development

Kingdom Ministry Systems

Growth Fundamentals

This tool gives you the fundamentals of growing in your faith and showing others how to do the same.

Subjects such as how to study the Bible, how to pray, how to be a witness for Christ, and more are included.

The Art of Disciplemaking

This tool details the art of disciplemaking and how every disciple of Christ can make disciples.

Analysis of the disciplemaking process is included as well as several different disciplemaking methods.

Leading and Multiplying Small Groups

This tool analyzes how to lead and multiply small groups.

Topics include leading and facilitating small group meetings, how to start and multiply groups and leaders, and more.

Systematic Theology

This tool evaluates systematic theology from a primarily Protestant perspective.

Topics such as the doctrine of God, Jesus, the Holy Spirit and more will be covered in a systematic and well-laid out manner.

Studies in Apologetics

This tool gives you the necessary apologetics tools to succcessfully defend your faith.

Subjects such arguments for the existence of God, defending the Resurrection of Christ, and much more are included.

Spiritual Gifts Inventory

This tool is a simple spiritual gifts inventory test that helps identify how God has gifted you.

The test gives you your top three spiritual gifts as well as an analysis of each gift and how they can be used in ministry.
