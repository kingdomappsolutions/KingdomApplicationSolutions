Mobile Apps to Help You Grow in Your Faith and to Lead Others to Jesus!

Mobile app development is one of the primary products Kingdom Application Solutions LLC offers. With the proclivity of mobile phones and their use, mobile apps provide an excellent medium to share the good news of Jesus and to help people grow as Christians or to help others engage in discipleship efforts.


App Products We Offer

Simple Evangelism

Simple Evangelism is an app that every follower of Jesus can use to lead people into a saving relationship with him. The app guides the user in a conversation with another through simple questions and a pleasant user interface.

Complete with common objections that people may offer, the app gives any Christian the confidence needed to share their faith. The app also offers a growth pathway for new believers that the user can follow to aid in initial discipleship efforts.

Evangelism should be easy. Not hard.

Simple Discipleship

Simple Discipleship is currently in production and is the companion app to Simple Evangelism. The app offers several simple discipleship methods that help believers make disciples who make disciples (Matt. 28:18-20).

Simple Discipleship will be offered in the app stores later this year.

Worldview Diagnostic

The Missiopologetic Worldview Diagnostic is also currently in production. This app is a tool that aids in diagnosing a person's worldview so that the Christian witness can better understand the person and share the gospel in a more productive way.

This app will be available at a later date. Other theology and apologetic tools will be released online as well to aid in having a solid theological background and the ability to effectively defend the faith.

Church & Ministry

We are currently developing various church and ministry apps to aid missionaries in fundraising, a prayer log for Sunday school classes, and a sermon notes app to aid in remembering the weekly message. These apps are designed to help churches and ministries empower their members to stay engaged in praying for one another and to grow in their faith.

These apps, once completed, will be released in the app stores.
