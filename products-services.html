The Products and Services We Offer

Presently, we offer two main products and services at Kingdom Application Solutions: mobile apps and websites for churches and/or ministries. Each of these products and services has the goal of leading others to faith in Christ and helping one another to grow in Christ-likeness. Click the link in each card below for more information for each of the products or services we offer.


Android & Apple Mobile Apps

We offer mobile apps that provide opportunities for churches, Christian ministries, or individual Christians to use day-to-day in their lives to aid in deeping their walk with Jesus as well as aiding in leading others to faith in Jesus. These mobile apps are developed for both Android and Apple and deployed to the Google Play Store and/or the Apple App Store.
