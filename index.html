Who We Are

We are a technology company that strives to glorify God through the creation and distribution of mobile apps and websites and providing website and IT consulting services. Our primary focus is churches, ministries, missionaries, and religious non-profit organizations but work with organizations of all sizes.

Our core values are:

  2. Integrity
  3. Service
Our Mission, Vision, & Values

Mobile App Development

We offer custom mobile app development primarily for churches, ministries, missionaries, and religious non-profit organizations at an affordable price as well as organizations of all sizes. Our goal is to bring mobile app development to those who otherwise would not be able to afford it. Our pricing packages seek to bridge the gap between having no mobile app and the high-priced apps.

Along with mobile app development, we also develop and deploy our own app solutions for both ministry purposes and entertainment value. Most of our apps are free with subscription options for more features. Each of our mobile apps are custom and tailored to your organization's needs. Our mobile apps are deployed to both the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store.

Website Development

We offer website design and development primarily to churches, ministries, missionaries/church planters, or religious non-profit organizations interested in having a quality-made website. Along with churches, we also cater to small businesses and organizations of any size. We offer the creation of websites through a content manangement option as well as more a more custom option if you desire. Our website development service is a monthly charge that includes development, site maintenance, SEO, regular updates, and much more.

We offer website consulting services to organizations of all sizes but focus more on churches, ministries, or religious non-profit organizations.
