About Our Company

Kingdom Application Solutions is a technology company that strives to glorify God through the creation and distribution of quality software applications and providing website and IT consulting services. We seek to aid any organization, small or large, that needs and desires mobile app development, website development, website consulting, and/or IT consulting services. We aim to make these services more accessible especially to churches, ministries, missionaries, and religious non-profit organizations. Our goal is to provide quality custom mobile apps and custom website development to deploy to your domain. Our user-friendly mobile apps will be deployed on both the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store. Our websites are hosted on a fast server with little to no downtime. We seek to help you along in developing or optimizing your own website through our website consulting services. Our IT consulting services can aid in any of your general IT-related issues such as security, hardware, or software selections.
