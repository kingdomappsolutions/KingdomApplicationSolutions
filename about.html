Our Company

Kingdom Application Solutions LLC is a software development company that strives to glorify God through the creation and distribution of quality mobile apps and quality website design & development for small businesses, non-profit organizations, and individuals. Our goal is to provide quality mobile apps and website development either through a content management system or with custom HTML to deploy to your domain. Our user-friendly mobile apps will be deployed on both the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store and our websites are hosted on a fast server with little to no downtime.


Our Founder

I have been developing for the web since the early 2000s and have been deploying mobile apps since 2019. I have designed multiple websites and managed content management systems for ministry organizations as well as deployed a number of mobile apps to the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store. I am passionate about designing quality mobile apps and developing user-friendly websites that glorify God and help people!
