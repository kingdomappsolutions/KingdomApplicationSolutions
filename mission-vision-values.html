The Mission, Vision, and Values of Kingdom Application Solutions LLC

Our Mission

Kingdom Application Solutions LLC exists to glorify God through the creation and distribution of quality mobile apps, custom website design & development, and graphic design work.


Our Vision

Kingdom Application Solutions LLC envisions a global impact from its products and services. We envision aiding small businesses, churches, ministries, missionaries, and other non-profit organizations in reaching their customers and congregations better to further their organizational goals and mission.


Our Values

Kingdom Application Solutions LLC has four main core values:

  Excellence in Everything

Excellence is when we demand more of ourselves than our clients do. Excellence in Everything means we go out of our way to ensure that every app or website developed is fully functional and provides a quality user experience. We will produce and deliver the best products and services we are capable of producing.

  Integrity and Honesty

Integrity and honesty are two sides of the same coin. To have integrity is to be honest and have strong moral principles. Integrity creates trust. When clients place their trust in us, it is because of our goal to maintain strong integrity and honesty. We will act in a trustworthy manner that earns the respect of our clients.

  Client Focused

Our company exists to help small businesses, churches, ministries, missionaries, and non-profit organizations have access to quality made websites, mobile apps, and graphic design. We make every decision and measure every outcome based on how well it serves our clients and how it builds the Kingdom. We listen to our clients’ needs, provide options, and deliver beyond their expectations.

  Accountability

Accountability is about staying true to our word. It means we do what we say we’re going to do. If something happens to prevent that, we determine the new course and communicate quickly. We believe that accepting responsibility for your actions (and inactions) is the ultimate way to build trust and also exemplifies our commitment to glorify God through our work.
