The Mission, Vision, and Values of Kingdom Application Solutions LLC

Our Mission

Kingdom Application Solutions LLC exists to glorify God through the creation and distribution of quality mobile apps and website design & development.


Our Vision

Kingdom Application Solutions LLC envisions a global impact from its mobile apps and website design and development. We envision aiding organizations and individuals in accomplishing their goals through quality software development.


Our Values

Kingdom Application Solutions LLC has four main core values:

Excellence in Everything

Excellence in everything means we go out of our way to ensure that every app or piece of software is fully functional and provides a quality user experience. Excellence reflects our commitment to glorify Christ and to do all things in his name (Col. 3:17).

Integrity and Honesty

Integrity and honesty are two sides of the same coin. To have integrity is to be honest and have strong moral principles. Being honest with strong moral principles demonstrates our devotion to Christ and to act honorably in all things (Heb. 13:18).

Christ Exalting

To exalt Christ in all things is the goal of every piece of software that we offer. This value drives our mission and vision so that Christ would be exalted and his name made great through the software we offer
(Phil. 2:9-10).

Kingdom Focused

As our name implies, we are a kingdom focused company that seeks to help organizations and individuals through the creation of software. Seeking his kingdom and growing the kingdom is what we are all about (Matt. 6:33)!
